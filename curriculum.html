Intro

From here you can decide whether you want to be a front-end or back-end developer.

It's very common for people to worry that the choice they make may not be the right one, that's why i suggest that you research a little (and i mean a little!) of everything just to get an idea of whats involved and how all the pieces fit together and choose from there.

Theres no need to sweat it, if you change your mind in the future, thats fine. More often than not, the skills you learn will very much be useful so have fun!

Its important to start by understanding roughly how the web works and where everything fits together. At this stage you're going to stock up on your theory. Listed below are some resources to get your juices flowing and your brain asking questions.
